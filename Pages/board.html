Meet The Board

The ACM Board is made up of students who are passionate about technology, learning, and helping others succeed. We work behind the scenes to plan events, host workshops, and create opportunities for students to connect with peers and professionals in the computing field. Whether you’re brand new to tech or an experienced programmer, our goal is to make ACM a welcoming place where everyone can grow and have fun along the way. For more details, join our Discord or GroupMe.
