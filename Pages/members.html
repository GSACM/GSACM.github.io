Meet The Members

ACM is more than just its leadership — it’s a community of students who share an interest in technology, creativity, and learning. Our members come from many different majors and experience levels, but they all share a curiosity for computing and a willingness to support one another. From collaborating on projects and attending workshops to exploring new tools and ideas, ACM members help make our organization an engaging and welcoming place for everyone. Whether you're writing your first line of code or already building advanced projects, there’s a place for you here. To meet more of our members and get involved, Discord or GroupMe.
